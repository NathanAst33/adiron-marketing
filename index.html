FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need a credit card to start? ▼
No. You get a full 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You only need to add payment info if you decide to continue after your trial ends.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime from your billing settings. When you cancel you keep full access until the end of your current billing period — we don't cut you off immediately.
What happens when my trial ends? ▼
You'll be prompted to choose a plan. If you don't subscribe your account will be locked but your data is saved — you won't lose anything if you come back later.
Can I change plans later? ▼
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade anytime from your billing settings through the Stripe portal.
What kind of contractors use Adiron? ▼
Any field service contractor — concrete, excavation, fencing, paving, landscaping, painting, pressure washing, and more. If you manage jobs, crew, and invoices, Adiron works for you.
How long does setup take? ▼
Most owners are up and running in under 30 minutes. You add your company info, create your first client and job, invite your crew, and you're done. Your crew needs to download nothing — they log in from any phone browser.
Do I need to train my crew to use it? ▼
Barely. The crew side is simple — they log in, clock in when they arrive, log hours, and clock out. Most crew members figure it out in under 5 minutes without any training.
Can my crew use it from their phones? ▼
Yes. Adiron is fully mobile-responsive and works on any smartphone or tablet. No app download required. Crew members can clock in and out, view their assigned jobs, and log hours straight from the job site.
Can I import my existing clients and jobs? ▼
Not automatically yet — that feature is on our roadmap. For now most owners add their active clients and jobs manually during setup which takes about 20 minutes.
Can clients pay invoices online? ▼
Yes. Invoices include an online payment button. Clients pay by card directly from the invoice link you send them. Payments are processed securely through Stripe and deposited into your bank account within 2 business days.
What does it cost to accept online payments? ▼
Adiron doesn't charge any additional fees for payments on top of your subscription. Stripe's standard processing fee applies — 2.9% plus 30 cents per transaction. On a $1,000 invoice you'd pay about $29.30 in processing fees. That's Stripe's fee, not ours.
Does it track equipment and vehicle maintenance? ▼
Yes — this is one of our strongest features. You can track maintenance logs, parts, scheduled maintenance, and get alerts when service is due for all your equipment and vehicles. Most tools at this price point don't have this.
Can my crew see my revenue and financial data? ▼
No. Crew members only see their own jobs, hours, and assigned tasks. They cannot see invoices, estimates, revenue, payroll reports, or any financial information. Only owners and managers have access to that.
How is Adiron different from Jobber? ▼
A few ways. First, price — we're about half the cost of Jobber for the same number of users. Second, we're built specifically for contractors running equipment — our equipment and vehicle maintenance tracking is built in, Jobber doesn't have that. Third, you're dealing directly with the founder, not a support ticket system.
Does Adiron integrate with QuickBooks? ▼
Not yet — that's our most requested feature and it's on the roadmap. If QuickBooks integration is a hard requirement for you today, we'd rather be honest about that than have you sign up and be disappointed. Email us at hello@getadiron.com and we'll let you know when it's ready.
What if I get stuck or something breaks? ▼
Email us at hello@getadiron.com or text us directly. You'll get a response from a real person, usually same day. We can also jump on a quick call and walk you through anything.
